Labelling Experiment

Requirements

  • Keyboard
  • Modern Web Browser such as:
    • Chrome 33+
    • Firefox 10+
    • Safari 6.1.6+
    • Internet Explorer 10+

Purpose

The purpose of this experiment is to explore methods for improving labelling experiences and the quality of those labels. You will be asked to label 45 tweets from a recent event (The "Sydney Siege"). This should take about 15 minutes.

All of the tweets are publicly available as of the time the experiment was posted.
